# Phase 3.3 Plan 01 Summary

Implemented taproot key-path verification on top of the existing
transaction-aware consensus surface.

## Accomplishments

- Extended the repo-owned sighash layer with BIP341 taproot hashing semantics,
  including annex-aware and `ANYONECANPAY` coverage.
- Extended `TransactionSignatureChecker` with Schnorr verification for
  `SigVersion::Taproot` and kept the verifier on the first-party consensus
  path.
- Routed witness-v1 key-path spends through the canonical `verify_input_script`
  witness dispatcher instead of introducing a parallel taproot verifier.
