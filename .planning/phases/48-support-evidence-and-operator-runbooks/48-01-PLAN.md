<threat_model>
- **Sensitive local data exposure:** support evidence may accidentally include
  RPC credentials, cookie contents, wallet private material, or raw log/wallet
  files. Mitigation: shape explicit evidence structs from redacted sources, do
  not archive raw files, and assert secret fixture strings are absent.
- **Source datadir mutation:** support collection could accidentally change
  config, wallet, service, or durable sync state. Mitigation: command only reads
  existing status/config/store/log/metrics artifacts and writes to the selected
  output directory.
- **False production claim:** a bundle could imply final public-mainnet proof or
  production-node readiness. Mitigation: docs and bundle summary distinguish
  deterministic local evidence from optional live-smoke artifacts and leave
  Phase 49/50 claims to later phases.
</threat_model>
