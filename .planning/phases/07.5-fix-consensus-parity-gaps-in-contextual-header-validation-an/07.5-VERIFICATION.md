### Gaps Summary

The original 07.5 parity work now exists and is wired correctly. The previous verification gaps are closed: explicit `current_time` flows through the peer-received block path, retarget-boundary and post-special min-difficulty recovery match Knots, the non-strict lax-DER path behaves correctly, and the final summary chain is truthful and authoritative.

No later roadmap phase clearly defers this work. Phase 8 is operator interfaces, Phase 9 is parity harnesses and fuzzing, and Phase 10 is benchmarks and audit readiness. This remains an actionable Phase 07.5 gap, not a deferred item.
