# Plan 39-01: Durable Sync Truth Across Daemon, CLI, Dashboard, And RPC

## Objective

Make daemon mainnet sync understandable and controllable by persisting durable sync state, projecting it through the existing operator status/dashboard surfaces, making RPC blockchain-info truthful during IBD, and adding explicit operator pause/resume control.

## Requirements

- PEERMAIN-04: Surface peer telemetry with source, negotiated identity, capabilities, contribution, failure reason, and last activity.
- CHAINMAIN-05: Surface resource pressure and actionable sync pressure truth.
- RESUME-04: Explain whether sync is active, paused, stopped, recovering, or failed.
- OBSMAIN-01: Show live sync state, peer count, best header/block heights, progress, lag, current phase, resource pressure, and last error.
- OBSMAIN-02: Route sync milestones and health through the existing metrics/log/status surfaces without changing retention contracts.
- OBSMAIN-03: Keep RPC and CLI blockchain-info truthful during IBD.
- OBSMAIN-04: Expose operator pause/resume and support evidence collection without store-file parsing.
- VERMAIN-01: Keep repo-native verification hermetic.
- VERMAIN-02: Add deterministic tests for the new truth and control surfaces.
